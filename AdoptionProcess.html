Adoption Process
We are excited that you're considering adopting

Thousands of animals come through the Paws & Whiskers Haven's doors every year, but no matter how they got here or where they came from, they're provided with shelter, nourishing food, individualized medical treatment, behavioral rehabilitation when necessary, and most important: love.

What to Bring

1. Any valid ID and proof of address (such as a utility bill or bank statement). You must be 18 years or older and sign an adoption contract. 

2. Children and dogs that live in your household (if applicable).  If you would like your child to meet an adoptable pet or your dog to be introduced to an adoptable dog before adopting, please bring them with you. We recommend dogs meet any resident dogs before adoption. All adults living in the home must also know about the pet and agree that it can live there.

3. Leash (dogs) or carrier (cats and small pets).  Required when taking a pet home for safety reasons. If you don't have these items, you can purchase from our Animal House pet supply shop at PWH Main.

The PWH may require other information or require other steps be taken prior to adoption to ensure a pet is the right match for your family. The PWH only adopts animals as family pets, not for consumption or research. The PWH reserves the right to deny any adoption for any reason.

Adoption Includes

  • A general physical exam by our medical staff, age-appropriate vaccinations, deworming, fecal testing, heartworm testing, and collar.
  • Free behavior and training advice.
